This project was a central security management centre for "vehicle-to-everything (V2X)" communications for a large national highway operator in South Korea. D'AMO KMS Hot Wallet and D'AMO KMS Cold Wallet previously named "PALLET Z" is a digital asset storage solution for enterprises and as a “digital safe” it is composed of a variety of interfaces designed to prevent theft of the assets in storage. Online wallets are prone to vulnerabilities and attacks from parties interested in the assets stored inside. One of the biggest problem is that a wallet is connected to the internet and by just simply disconnecting it, the whole problem with cyber attacks is gone. But (there is always a but) what if someone has physical access? This is where the PALLET Z digital asset storage solution come to protect.
